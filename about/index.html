TEST BLAH BLAH Hi, that's me. I'm a post-doctoral research assistant at the Unversity College London working in the field of extrasolar planet characterisation. I am currently funded through the European Research Council grant (Exolights, PI: Tinetti) for which I act as the scientific coordinator.

My research interests range from Infra-red detector characterisation and modelling to data de-trending using machine learning algorithms to radiative transfer modelling of exoplanetary atmospheres. I also have a keen interest to go into the development of data anaylsis techniques for direct imagine of extrasolar planets in the future. You can find a summary of my research in the My Research section and a summary of my current projects in the Current Projects section.
